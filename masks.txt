== !! LEAST protection but easy breathing - best for outdoors. Not recommended at closed space !! ==
- Senovia Pro:  This mask is an upgraded cloth mask with Zinc embded in the fabric which suppose to kill viruses.

                Source -> https://twitter.com/masknerd/status/1484027230409928704?s=21&t=kLm_lnZYWvjP9qYNqENEMA
                
                Add another mask beneath it - i.e double mask - sergical/plain mask + the Senovia Pro

- SN98:         This is an Israeli made high quality plain mask. I use it with the Senovia Pro. **Double Masking**
